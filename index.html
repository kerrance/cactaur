The Highwind is the stolen airship acquired by Cid Highwind in Final Fantasy VII, and serves as a means of transportation for the entire party. Although it has no visible weapons, it is used during Cid's Limit Break named Highwind, where several missiles are launched from the ship.

The Ragnarok is the primary airship, created by the nation of Esthar during the Sorceress War, it is heavily armed and capable of space travel. It was originally used, along with two others, to banish Sorceress Adel to space.

The Fahrenheit is the airship available to the player in Final Fantasy X.
